We are COMMANDOS have endeavored to discover a solution to healthy problems in the hope that we will improve the health of our society in which we live in order to create a society with a better Health.

What We Did

The Problem

The daily movement of blind people in the streets is difficult, as when they walk in the street they are at risk of colliding with objects, walls or poles that are on their way, as this makes their lives much more difficult and their execution of tasks takes longer, Given the importance of this subject, after research and reflection, our team has reached The idea of ​​making a tool that is placed on the head contains an AI camera vision as the one which is placed in the modern smart cars, it's programmed by the Ai so, we want to understand more how it works so that we can create a smart device that contains this camera, and we also think of linking it to a mobile application or a website which we already are working on it, that keeps data and gives notifications or rings the user, and we also plan to have a head phone or something to inform the user so that if there is something approaching, it tells him immediately before approaching him, mentioning the type of object that is approaching, whether it is a human, a car, an animal or a pole. Thus, we have tried to solve this problem faced by the blind.
